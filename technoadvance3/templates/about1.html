About Us

Diginique, A unit of DiGi Infy is a fast growing organization driven by passion towards the betterment of students in the field of technology and management. It is led by a group of intellectuals who have been working day and night to refine the advancement in the field of education. Our services bridge the gap of campus to corporate so that no any participant is left behind in this era of cut throat competition.

We provide techno-managerial workshops on various topics like: Android App Development, Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IOT), Ethical Hacking and Cyber Security, Digital Marketing, etc. especially designed for enthusiastic learners in the best way to gain practical knowledge in minimum time like two days and the students get hands on experience session by industry experts.


Our Philosophy:

Delivering unique content and providing an excellent training is the philosophy of our visionaries. We expect participants to design their talent and to move on the path paved for them.

Our Future:

Diginique envisions a future full of enthusiasm, overwhelmed with technology, and dealt with proper management. Our motive lies in dedication towards perfection and perfection towards achievement.
